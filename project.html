About

Meridian icecream

Since 1999, Meridian Ice Cream has been an acclaimed brand in Pune, known for its rich and velvety ice creams that captivate every palate. With more than 35 unique and indulgent flavors, our dedication to quality has earned us a loyal following, driving the success of over 34 outlets and growing.


Meridian Ice Cream offers one of the most prestigious franchise opportunities in India. With exceptional support, a proven history of success, and a respected brand reputation, we provide a solid foundation for motivated entrepreneurs. Ready to join a brand that’s built on flavor, growth, and success? Become part of the Meridian family and help shape a legacy of extraordinary taste and lasting business success.
